# Browsing strats

The **Browse** page is the home screen. Everyone on the team can use it — no login required.

## Cards

Each **card** is one strat. You’ll see:

- **Title** — what the round is called  
- **Map** and **side** — Attack (red tone) or Defense (blue tone)  
- **Difficulty** — three bars (1 = simplest, 3 = most demanding)  
- **Agents** — who the plan expects (chips on the card)  
- A **short summary** — tap or click the card to open the full view  

## Opening a strat

Click a card to open the **detail panel**. There you can see:

- **Images** — swipe or use arrows if there are multiple  
- **Summary** — the full text blurb  
- **Round plan** — numbered steps (coaches can emphasize names with bold text)  
- **Roles** — who does what  
- **Coach notes** — extra callouts (often highlighted)  

Press **Escape** or click outside the panel to close it.

## Filters

- **Search** — matches title, map, agents, tags, and notes.  
- **Map chips** — built from the strats you have; **All** clears the map filter.  
- **Side** — **All sides**, **Attack**, or **Defense** only.  

If nothing shows up, filters may be too narrow — try **All** map and **All sides**, or clear the search box.
